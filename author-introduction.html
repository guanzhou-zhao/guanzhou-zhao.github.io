Why are you attending Enspiral Dev Academy?

I am attending EDA for skills and techniques I need to enter the local market. I arrived NZ in May of 2015 from China and was to find a developing job and settle down here. I majored in Computer Network in college and have got some programming language knowledge. After graduation, I joined HP worked as software tester and hardware supporter, meanwhile I picked up more programming skills. Before I came here I had 7 months developing work on Java Web. That's a period of tough time. I had some interviews for developing job, but all failed. Just before I determined to go back China to have more work experience and maybe come back again, I heard about EDA from a friend. Immediately I looked through EDA's website and found that's what I need. I don't need to think about one more time and determined to enroll in. I was afraid I might be refused, but I got this chance to study with my cohorts.
