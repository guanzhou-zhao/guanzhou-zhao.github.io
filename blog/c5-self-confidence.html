What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

They are essentially the same.

Self awareness is a neutral mode that maintains self-reflectiveness even in the midst of turbulent emotions. --Daniel Goleman

Mindfulness means paying attention in a particular way: on purpose, in the present moment, and non-judgementally. --Jon Kabat-Zinn

They are both about knowing what I am doing with meta-attention.

Try at least one of the self awareness practices from the chapter (Body Scan, Journaling). What was your experience of it? How did you feel?

I have been employing Body Scan technique to make me fall into sleep fast and also to relax myself. I should hear about this technique from somewhere else, but don't remember exactly where.

I felt that it was a perfect technique for falling into sleep quickly. And now I understand that it is also leading to emotional awareness and to self-awareness.
