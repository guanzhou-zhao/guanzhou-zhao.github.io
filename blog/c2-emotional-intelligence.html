What learnings did you take from the chapter?

Emotional inteligence is in two part. First, intrapersonal. Second, interpersonal. In detail, intropersonal includes self-awareness, self-regulation, motivation; interpersonal includes emphathy and social skills.

Emotional intelligence leads to stella work performance, outstanding leadership and the ability to create the conditions for happiness.

Search Inside Yourself's approach to cultivating emotional intelligence begins with mindfulness.
