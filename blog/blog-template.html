The comparison doesn’t matter. What matters is that you have engaged with the material, and given it a go. If you've been holding attention, you'll have learnt a lot, even if it doesn't feel like it. If you’ve given it a good shot, and are still struggling, get help ASAP from your cohort - if that's not helping unblock you, message your coaches and move on. Your focus is learning, not producing products (yet). Make the most of it. Time boxing is a great tool to stay focussed on learning, and move onto other learnings rather than falling into perfecting a product.
